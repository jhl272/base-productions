Frequently Asked Questions

What is the time commitment?
Base's time commmitmennt is flexible depending on how many dances you choose to dance in. Each dance practices for 1 hour/week with additional practices before our showcase.
Can I audition for both types of dance?
Yes! You are more than welcome to stay for both portions of the audition day.
Do you get nervous before performances?
We have found it super common among dancers to get butterflies before performances but this only helps elevate our performance on stage.
