Meet our Team

Board Members

Get to know some of our members! Base is a warm and welcoming family. See how some of our members got to know Base and how it influenced their college experience.

Lena Diamond: Co-President

Hello! My name is Lena Diamond and I am one of the Co-Presidents of BASE. I am a senior this year and study Psychology and Design and Environmental Analysis. The first time I encountered BASE was my freshman year, when I was handed a quarter card on Ho plaza about auditions. I tried out for a different dance team and was told BASE would be a better fit for me. So since freshman year I have been a member of BASE and am so beyond grateful to have had this team throughout my years of college. Being a part of a community like BASE is a very comforting thing to have when you're new to college. My teammates are amazing friends as well as incredible dancers. It means so much to me to be able to lead and represent this team in my last year at Cornell. No matter what, BASE is always there when I am angry, sad or overwhelmed, and it is the type of group of people that will continue to be there for me even after I graduate.

Taylor Luck: Vice President

Hello! My name is Taylor and I am the VP of BASE . I am a Junior this year and study Information Science and Communication. The first time I encountered BASE was when I was a first semester freshman. It has been a huge part of my college experience. I spend countless hours working on dances and organizing the team and its activities. I have made some of my best friends by joining the team.
